Que ) Write a prompt to summarize a news article into 2 sentences. If the summary exceeds 50 words, truncate it to the nearest complete sentence.

Ans) 
You are a professional news editor with experience of more than 10 years.

Summarize the following news article in exactly "2 clear sentences".
The summary must be concise, factual, and neutral.
Do not exceed "50 words total".
Do not add opinions or extra context.
